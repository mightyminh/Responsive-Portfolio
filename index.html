About Me

I am Minh Tuyền Lê Mai aka Mighty Minh, a resilient supernatural funky force to be reckoned with. I like catching Pokémon, understanding people's sense of the world, and saving public education from total collapse. Currently, I am a research specialist at the Wisconsin HOPE Lab. I have an M.A. in Educational Policy Studies, a triple B.S. in Sociology, Southeast Asian Studies, Languages & Cultures of Asia, and a certificate in Asian American Studies — all from the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I also consider myself a Minh of many talents. Get in touch with me if you need a website for your own academic profile, help with Vietnamese translation or making a Prezi.
